## 🗓️ Daily Goal (Day 1 to 10)

> Stick to this for just 10 days — and you’ll surprise yourself. ✨

| 🕒 Time            | 🎯 Task                                                        |
| ------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5:30 AM – 6:00 AM  | 🌅 Wake up early + drink water                                 |
| 6:00 AM – 6:30 AM  | 🧘 Light exercise / meditation                                 |
| 6:30 AM – 9:00 AM  | 📘 Study Block 1 – Concept Building (Physics/Polity/Math)      |
| 9:00 AM – 9:30 AM  | 🍽️ Breakfast break                                            |
| 9:30 AM – 12:00 PM | 🧪 Study Block 2 – NCERT reading or notes (Bio/Geography/Chem) |
| 1:00 PM – 3:00 PM  | 🧠 Mock test / PYQs / Topic-wise practice                      |
| 4:00 PM – 5:30 PM  | ✍️ Review + error logbook                                      |
| 7:00 PM – 8:00 PM  | 📺 Watch topper strategy / lectures                            |
| 9:00 PM – 9:30 PM  | 📓 Light revision + next day planning                          |

<Info title="Note on Study Blocks">
The subjects mentioned are examples. Adjust them based on your specific exam (NEET, JEE, UPSC) and your weak/strong areas. The key is consistent, focused effort in each block.
</Info>

## ✅ Streak Rules

* 📅 **Log your day** in your app/planner before bed.
* 🚫 **No phone distractions** during study blocks. (Use app blockers if needed!)
* 🔁 **Missed a day?** Restart from Day 1 — no excuses! This builds true discipline.
* 📊 Track in a **heatmap calendar** (like GitHub streaks). Many apps offer this.
* 🏆 Unlock **“Focused Learner”** badge after completing the streak! (If your app supports it)

---

## 📌 Bonus Tips to Stay Consistent

*   Use the **Pomodoro Timer (25–5)** to avoid burnout. Our app has a built-in one!
*   Join a **WhatsApp/Telegram accountability group** with serious aspirants.
*   Set wallpaper reminders on your phone/laptop: "Day X/10 — Don’t Break the Chain! 🚀"
*   Prepare your study space and materials the night before to reduce morning friction.

<Tip title="Hydration and Nutrition">
Don't forget to drink plenty of water and eat healthy meals. Your brain needs fuel to perform optimally during intense study sessions!
</Tip>
