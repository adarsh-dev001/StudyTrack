The final phase of your NEET preparation as a dropper is crucial. This intensive 7-day study plan is designed to help you consolidate your knowledge, sharpen your problem-solving skills, and build exam temperament. Remember, consistency and smart work are key!

<Info title="Plan Overview">
This plan assumes you have completed the NEET syllabus at least once. The focus is on rigorous revision, extensive MCQ practice, PYQ analysis, and strategic mock test attempts. Adapt it to your specific strengths and weaknesses.
</Info>

## üéØ Key Assumptions & How to Use This Plan

*   **Syllabus Coverage:** You have a working knowledge of all subjects and topics.
*   **Revision Focus:** Prioritize revising high-yield topics, formulas, and concepts you find challenging.
*   **MCQ Practice:** Solve a significant number of MCQs daily to improve speed and accuracy.
*   **Mock Tests:** Incorporate full-length and subject-wise mock tests. **Crucially, analyze every mock test thoroughly.**
*   **PYQs:** Dedicate time to solve and understand Previous Year Question Papers.
*   **Flexibility:** This is a template. Adjust timings and topics based on your energy levels and weak areas.
*   **Health is Wealth:** Ensure 7-8 hours of sleep, timely healthy meals, and short breaks to avoid burnout. üßòüçΩÔ∏èüò¥
